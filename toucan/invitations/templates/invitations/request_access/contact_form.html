Request access to Toucan

Toucan is currently invitation only.

Since the information shared on Toucan is potentially sensitive in nature we require some checks before you will be able to access the application.

There are currently 2 ways to sign up for Toucan:

  1. Get an invitation from your organisation if they are already using Toucan. Just ask an existing member to invite you to their organisation. You will then receive an invitation email and be able to signup as a member of that organisation.
  2. Fill out the form below to contact us and we will get back to you as soon as possible. Please write a short paragraph on why you want to participate.
