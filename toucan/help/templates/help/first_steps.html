Get notified on what is relevant to you

e.g. you are only interested in medical issues in your geographic radius

On Toucan you are notified when another organisation creates an issue. In order to avoid an overflow of notifications, you can select for which organisation and what kind of issue you want to be notified on in a geographic radius. If an issue matching your criteria is created you will be notified immediately and can help resolve it.

Create New Notification Rule

Choose how you want to be notified

e.g. When another user directly mentions your name you want to receive a text. If a user mentions the organisation you work for you only want to receive an email.

On Toucan you can reach individuals and organisations that would otherwise not be able see your requests for help. Therefore a user can directly mention a person or an organization. In order to make sure you only get notified on what you want to be notified on, choose what kind of notification you receive when you or your organisation get mentioned.

Create Mentioning Rule

Add an Issue

You have a problem and need the support of other organisations.

Issues can be added by clicking “Add issue” on the header menu. You will be directed to an issue creation form to briefly describe your issue. After you click "create" your issue will be visible on the map of all issues and organizations relevant to the issue will be notified.

Add an issue

Resolve an Issue

Directly communicate with other users and resolve an issue on Toucan

On Toucan you can directly comment on issues and get in touch with other users to solve problems as efficiently as possible. You can upload pictures or files for greater detail and also directly mention another organization or user by typing '@'and their name. Once the issue has been solved, you can click on 'Resolve Issue' and it will disappear from the list of issues on Toucan.
